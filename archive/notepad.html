Vectors Review

Given \(|\vec{a}| = 6\) and \(|\vec{b}| = 10\), angle between \(\vec{a}\) and \(\vec{b}\) = 100*, find magnitude of \(\vec{s} = \vec{a} + \vec{b}\) and angle between \(\vec{a}\) and \(\vec{s}\)

\(|\vec{s}|^2 = |\vec{a}|^2 + |\vec{b}|^2 + 2|\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\cos\theta = \sqrt{6^2 + 10^2 + 2(6)(10)cos(100)} = \sqrt{115.162} = 10.731\)
